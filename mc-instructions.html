Mike Cross' Favorite Splits

5 personal splits · 18 workouts · Multiple set styles per session

Program Rules
Sets: All exercises completed between 3–5 sets
Rest: 30 seconds to 1 minute between sets
Cardio: Incline treadmill + jump rope — see cardio page after each workout
Variation: Any workout can be completed as pyramid sets, time under tension, drop sets, or low reps
