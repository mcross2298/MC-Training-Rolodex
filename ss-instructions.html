📋 Program Guide

Strength & Supersets

6-week cycle · 5 training days · heavy compounds + high-volume supersets

💡 Tip: tap the "FLAGSHIP" badge at the top of the program page to jump straight back into your last in-progress workout — no need to scroll through the split list.
Daily Structure
3 Heavy Compounds: 4–8 reps each, at least one run as a pyramid (weight climbs as reps drop)
2 Supersets: 3–4 sets, 12–30 reps — paired exercises back to back, no rest between A and B
1 AMRAP Finisher: as many reps as possible, target 50–100 reps
Tempo: not prescribed by default — an optional add-in, applied per-exercise via the ⋮ menu
Badge Key
⚡ SupersetTwo exercises back to back, no rest between
📈 PyramidWeight increases as reps decrease (12,10,8,8)
🏋️ Low RepHeavy load, low reps, strength focus (5×5, 6×4)
🔥 High RepPump-focused, high volume (4×20, 3×30)
💀 AMRAPAs many reps as possible — push the finisher to failure
Add Tempo — Optional Intensifier
⏱️Tap the ⋮ on any exercise → Add Tempo
Tempo is a serviceable add-in, not prescribed. Too easy? Slow the lift down to cut reps and raise output (e.g. 12 reps at 110 lb on goblet squats → add a 3-sec negative). Read as Eccentric : Pause bottom : Concentric : Pause top.
⏱ 3:0:1:0
3 sec lower · explosive lift up
⏱ 4:0:1:0
4 sec negative · controlled lift
⏱ 3:1:1:0
3 sec lower · 1 sec pause at bottom · 1 sec lift
⏱ 2:0:1:2
2 sec lower · 1 sec lift · 2 sec squeeze at top
Program Overview
🦵Legs
Squats, RDLs and pyramid work leading into paired quad/calf and hamstring/glute supersets, finished with a leg curl AMRAP.
💪Chest
Heavy pressing and pyramid work into fly/press supersets, finished with a push-up ladder or bench burnout.
🏋️Back & Shoulders / Arms & Forearms / Cardio & Calves
Same daily shape — 3 compounds → 2 supersets → 1 AMRAP finisher — applied across the rest of the 5-day split.
