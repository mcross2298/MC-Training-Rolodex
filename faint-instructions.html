📋 Not for the Faint of Heart · Guide

Not for the Faint of Heart — Mike Cross

9 workouts · Timed rounds · Ladders · AMRAP · Battle Ropes · Boxing

Program Philosophy
Designer: Mike Cross — these are conditioning and mental toughness workouts. Not traditional lifting. Push until you can't.
Format: Timed rounds, rep ladders, and gauntlets. Most workouts have no fixed set count — you work within the structure and push maximally.
Rest: Strictly prescribed per workout. 30 sec between rounds in boxing, 30 sec on/off in battle ropes. Don't skip the structure.
AMRAP: When an exercise says "to failure" — that means complete collapse. Last rep is the one you physically cannot complete.
Signature Formats
🪜 Ladder SetsSet 1 = 1 rep, Set 2 = 2 reps... Set 18 = 18 reps. Total = up to 171 reps. When you fail, switch to knee variation and continue.
⏱️ Timed Rounds3 minutes on, 30 sec rest. Used for jump rope, heavy bag, shadow boxing, burpees, and conditioning circuits.
💀 AMRAPGo until you physically cannot move. Sit-ups, quad extensions, split squats. No rep target — complete failure is the goal.
⚡ Gauntlet30s or 1min On/OffBattle Ropes format — 8 movements in sequence, 2 rounds. Short rest between each movement.
🔁 EMOMEvery Minute on the Minute. 25 push-ups (Basic/Intermediate) or 8 burpees (Advanced L3) at the start of each minute.
📈 Pyramid RoundsFull Body Pyramid — timed rounds (90s-2min per exercise) or ascending/descending rep pyramids.
Battle Ropes Levels
🟢Basic
30s on / 30s off · 2 rounds · EMOM 25 push-ups after all 8 rope sets · 5 min jump rope finisher
🟡Intermediate
1 min on / 30s off · 2 rounds · EMOM 25 push-ups after all 8 rope sets · 5 min jump rope finisher
🟠Advanced L1
30s on / 1 min off · 2 rounds · EMOM 20 push-ups between each of the 8 rope sets · 5 min jump rope finisher
🔴Advanced L2
1 min on / 1 min off · 2 rounds · EMOM 20 push-ups between each of the 8 rope sets · 5 min jump rope finisher
💀Advanced L3
30s on / 1 min off · 2 rounds · EMOM 8 burpees between each of the 8 rope sets · 5 min jump rope finisher
Workouts
🏋️The 500
Leg day meets conditioning. Walking lunge ladder (1-18 = 300 lunges) + push-up ladder (1-18 = up to 171 push-ups). Target: 150+ total push-ups.
🚗Driveway Demolition
Push-up 1-18 ladder + core circuit. 15 sec rest between sets. Optional 5 min jump rope x2.
😈Hell Week
~90-120 min. Treadmill → 15 min jump rope → 10 rounds burpees → 100 lunges → repeat twice more → 2,000-4,000m row → 10 min sauna.
⏱️45-Minute Burner
Incline treadmill + jump rope + 10 rounds of burpees (1 min on / 30 sec rest). Pure conditioning.
💪Popeye
Forearm isolation circuit. Wrist rolls, reverse curls, DB twists. 30s on / 30s off. 5-10 lb dumbbells. ~20-30 min.
🔥Turn & Burn
15 min jump rope + choice of 10-round burpee EMOM or 1-18 push-up ladder. Two options for the second phase.
📈Full Body Pyramid
Timed pyramid rounds (90s-2min per exercise) or rep-based ascending/descending pyramid. Multiple round formats.
🥊Boxing Routine
3 phases: Jump rope + heavy bag (3x3 min) → conditioning circuit (5x3 min: push-ups, burpees, jumping jacks, shadow boxing, walk the line) → sit-ups AMRAP + 1-2 mile run.
🪢Battle Ropes
8-movement gauntlet across 5 levels. Alternating waves, double waves, snake waves, clockwise/counter-clockwise. EMOM work between sets. Jump rope finisher.
