The Modality Matrix

15 weeks · 3 phases · one equipment type isolated per phase

Three Phases
🏋️Phase 1 — Dumbbell (Weeks 1–5)
Pure DB isolation, built entirely at a single adjustable bench — the DB/Bench Anchor archetype.
🔩Phase 2 — Barbell & Smith (Weeks 6–10)
Olympic-barbell compounds stand alone, never supersetted. Grouped blocks are single-bar EZ/short-barbell complexes. Smith lives only in the cluster and drop positions — exactly 2 Smith lifts a day — and never shares a block with an Olympic barbell.
⚡Phase 3 — Cable & Plate-Loaded (Weeks 11–15)
Cable-dominant. Grouped blocks are pure single-cable-column complexes — one stack, swap attachments. Plate-loaded machines are standalone, at exactly 3 spots a day (the heavy anchor, the cluster, and the drop).
Every Day Is Complete
Each training day carries all 7 intensifiers — low-rep, high-rep, TUT, tri-set, superset, cluster and drop — across a 2 / 4 / 4 working-set mix, every single week. A week's theme never strips an intensifier out of the day; it just rides visibly on 4 feature lifts while 6 anchors hold their scheme.
