The Dashboard — your home base

Three zones, one bottom tab bar. Learn these and you can reach anything in the app in one or two taps.

  • ✓Current Program hero card — pin the program you're running and it sits front-and-center with its splits, phase pills, and a ring showing where you are. Tap “See all” to swap it anytime; it remembers your pick.
  • ✓Training Tools grid — Exercise Library, Workout Logs, Build Your Own, Program Guide, and the Quick Tour. Your power tools, all in one block.
  • ✓Bottom tabs — ⚡ Dashboard · 🏋️ Programs · 🔥 Conditioning. That bar follows you everywhere, so you can't get lost.
  • ✓Smart resume — leave mid-session and a resume banner brings you right back where you left off, every set intact.

4
The logging engine — the heartbeat

Every workout in every program runs on the same core loop: log the set, rest on the clock, beat last time, bank the PR.

  • ✓Log Sets — tap it under any exercise, punch in weight and reps, tap the ✓. The set locks in and your prescribed rest timer auto-starts. Drop-set and AMRAP rows appear automatically when the program calls for them.
  • ✓“Last: X lb · date” on every card — your built-in cue to progressively overload. You always know what to beat.
  • ✓Superset timers — paired exercises get their own rhythm: quick hop between movements, full rest after the pair.
  • ✓Live stat strip — check your first set and a sticky strip wakes up with session duration and sets logged. Tap Summary anytime to watch the session take shape.
  • ✓Finish Workout — saves the session and auto-detects personal records. Beat a previous best and it's tagged with a gold 🏆 PR. No manual tracking, ever.
  • ✓Workout Logs — three running totals up top (Workouts · Sets Logged · PRs Set) over your full dated history of every weight you actually moved. Proof of work, and your roadmap for next time.
  • ✓Rep progression — per-exercise history across sessions, so you can see the line going up.

This is where the app earns its keep. Punch in the work, let the timers run the rests, and watch the PRs stack up — the record keeps itself.

5
Make it yours

No program survives contact with a crowded gym — so every exercise bends to you, and you can build sessions from scratch.

  • ✓The ⋮ menu — every exercise card carries a control panel: 🔁 Replace any movement from the Library, ↕️ Reorder the lineup, ⏱️ Add Tempo (eccentric:pause:concentric:pause, shown as a pill), and 📝 Notes to your future self. A dot on the ⋮ flags cards with saved notes or tempo. Everything sticks per workout.
  • ✓Build Your Own — name a workout, search or filter the library by muscle group, tap exercises into your tray, set sets & reps, and hit Create. It lands under My Custom Workouts and logs sets and PRs exactly like the built-ins.
  • ✓Exercise Library — 500+ movements grouped by muscle, with real-time search and tap-to-expand variations.
  • ✓Program Guide — the methodology, set styles, and rep ranges behind every program, so you always know the why.
6
The nutrition suite — fuel the work

The 🍎 Nutrition tab is a full macro tracker built into the same app, so training and eating live side by side. Set your targets, log your food, and watch the rings fill — day by day.

  • ✓Daily macro dashboard — calories, protein, fat and carbs tracked as rings that fill toward your goals, with a tappable week calendar so every day keeps its own log.
  • ✓Goal calculator — set calorie and macro targets by hand, or auto-calculate them, from the ⚙ gear. The rings measure against whatever you set.
  • ✓Food search + barcode scanner — look up foods in a large database, or tap the ▦ icon and scan a label to pull the full nutrition in automatically — no typing.
  • ✓Tappable nutrition-facts sheet — every food opens to a full facts panel: the macro breakdown, micronutrients, a grams / oz unit toggle, and a keypad to enter your exact amount or a multiple (e.g. 3× a 2-tbsp serving). Macros recalculate live as you adjust.
  • ✓Favorites library — star the foods you eat most and save meals, then quick-log them in two taps from the ★ Favorites library instead of searching every time.

Strength is built in the gym and revealed in the kitchen. Set your numbers once, log as you go, and the Nutrition tab keeps your fuel honest with the work you're putting in.

7
Your data, everywhere you train

Offline-first by design, cloud-synced when you want it, locked down where it counts.

  • ✓Installs like a native app — Add to Home Screen and it launches full-screen, no browser bar. Dead gym signal? Programs, logging, and PRs keep working. A green 🔄 Update available banner lets you refresh on your terms.
  • ✓On-device first — logs, PRs, custom workouts, notes, tempo, and your macro logs & food favorites all save locally. Private and instant.
  • ✓Invite-only accounts + cross-device sync — sign in from the dashboard avatar and your training data follows you across devices. No public signup, no sign-in nag.
  • ✓Face ID / Touch ID — biometric, device-bound unlock gates the Program Manager, where the owner can edit and customize programs directly.
