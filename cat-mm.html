Three phases. Three modalities. One system — pure dumbbell isolation locks in mind-muscle mastery before the barbell builds raw strength, then cables finish with constant-tension conditioning.

Every Day Is Complete. Each training day carries all 7 intensifiers — low-rep, high-rep, TUT, tri-set, superset, cluster and drop — across a 2 / 4 / 4 working-set mix, every single week. Each phase isolates one equipment type and runs 5 themed weeks; the week's dominant style visibly rides on just 4 feature lifts while 6 anchors hold their scheme so no intensifier ever drops out: Week 1: Low-Rep · Week 2: Pyramid · Week 3: Tempo · Week 4: High-Rep · Week 5: Superset.
